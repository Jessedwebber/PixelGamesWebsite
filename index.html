Klondike & Spider

Two beloved solitaire variants in one app. Classic Klondike lets you choose between Draw 1 and Draw 3 modes, while Spider Solitaire offers three difficulty levels with 1, 2, or 4 suits.

DRAW 1 & DRAW 3 MODES SPIDER: 1, 2 OR 4 SUITS TAP-TO-MOVE CONTROLS

Every card is hand-crafted pixel art. Drag cards naturally or tap to auto-move them — the game feels as smooth on a phone as it does on a tablet.

Track Your Stats

Keep tabs on your performance with detailed statistics for every game mode. See your win rate climb, chase your best time, and track total games played.

GAMES WON & WIN RATE BEST TIME & AVERAGE TIME UNDO TRACKING

Stats are tracked separately for Klondike and Spider, so you can measure your progress in each mode independently. A pixel-art trophy celebrates every victory.

Retro Pixel Art

Every element is lovingly crafted in a retro pixel art style inspired by classic 8-bit and 16-bit era games. From the neon-glow TRON grid backdrop to the hand-drawn card faces, the game drips with nostalgic charm.

HAND-CRAFTED PIXEL CARDS ANIMATED TRON GRID CRT SCANLINE OVERLAY

Cascading card-back animations, a glowing horizon line, and subtle jelly physics make the landing screen feel alive. It's solitaire like you've never seen it.

4 Difficulties

Whether you're just learning or you're a seasoned Sudoku master, there's a challenge waiting. Choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, or Expert puzzles — each carefully generated for a fair, solvable experience.

EASY — GREAT FOR BEGINNERS MEDIUM — A BALANCED CHALLENGE HARD — TEST YOUR LOGIC EXPERT — FOR TRUE MASTERS

Every puzzle is uniquely generated with a single valid solution. Difficulty scales by reducing the number of given cells and requiring more advanced solving techniques.

Pencil Marks

Toggle notes mode to jot down candidate numbers in any empty cell. Small pencil marks appear in a neat 3x3 grid inside the cell, just like writing lightly in the corner of a paper puzzle.

TAP TO ADD/REMOVE CANDIDATES NEAT 3x3 MINI-GRID LAYOUT TOGGLE NOTES MODE ON/OFF

Pencil marks are essential for harder puzzles. Use them to narrow down possibilities, then commit to a number when you're sure. They're automatically cleaned up when you place a final digit.

Best Times

Race against yourself with a built-in timer on every puzzle. Your best completion times are saved per difficulty level so you can track your improvement over time.

TIMER ON EVERY GAME BEST TIME PER DIFFICULTY WIN RATE & GAMES PLAYED

The HUD shows your current time, move count, and error count in real time. After winning, a victory screen displays your final stats with a pixel-art trophy.
